midnight crew: escape from boredom

what?

a community created with the sole purpose of escaping boredom.

why?

because we were bored.

when?

we started somewhere around march when the quaratines were being
applied.

about midnight crew

midnight crew is a group of friends who are just seeking for an escape from
the boredom they go through everyday. from playing games to watching movies
and other sorts of stuff they always try to come up with new ways of having fun.

in the early days of the midnight crew it was raised by a single person who was trying
to have some fun, but it quickly became a thing where everyone contributed to the group.
